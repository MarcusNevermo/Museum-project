Welcome to the Ant Room!

Step into the fascinating world of ants and discover the incredible lives of these tiny engineers.

Our Ant Room features a state-of-the-art, transparent ant farm that showcases the complex underground tunnels and chambers these industrious insects build.

Step Back in Time: The Mammoth Experience

Journey to the Ice Age and encounter the mighty mammoth, a towering giant of the prehistoric world.

Our Mammoth Room immerses you in the world of these magnificent creatures with a lifelike, full-scale mammoth replica standing majestically at the center

Unleashing the Power of Particles: The Accelerator Experience

Step into the cutting-edge world of particle physics with our Particle Accelerator Exhibition!

This immersive display takes you behind the scenes of the world's most advanced scientific instruments.

Dive into Virtual Realities: The VR Experience

Step into the future of exploration with our Virtual Reality (VR) Room, where the boundaries of imagination and technology blend seamlessly.

Put on a VR headset and transport yourself to extraordinary worlds, from the depths of the ocean to distant galaxies, and even historical landmarks.
